4. Description of the Service

Convenu is an all-in-one trip planning and networking tool that provides:

  • Itinerary Planning: Create, edit, and manage travel itineraries with events, transit details, and metrics.
  • Contact Management (CRM): Store and organize professional contacts with notes, tags, and export to CSV.
  • Itinerary Sharing: Share itineraries with other users via links, with visibility controls.
  • Event Import: Import events from your Google Calendar (read-only access) and from Luma (lu.ma) event URLs.
  • Telegram Bot: Create and manage contacts, itineraries, and events via the @convenubot Telegram bot. Forward messages to automatically create contacts.
  • Bulk Invite: Compose personalized messages and open Telegram DMs to send to your contacts. Messages are not sent automatically; you initiate each send.
  • Proof of Handshake: A blockchain-based feature that creates verifiable records of in-person meetings via compressed NFTs on the Solana network.
  • Trust Scores and Points: A reputation system based on verified handshakes and profile signals.
  • Dashboard: Overview of your handshakes, points, and trust metrics.
  • Print/Export: Export itineraries as printable PDFs.

5.3 Proof of Handshake

  • Proof of Handshake is a feature that mints a compressed NFT (cNFT) on the Solana blockchain when two users verify an in-person meeting.
  • Each party pays a transaction fee of approximately 0.01 SOL per handshake to cover on-chain costs.
  • Pending handshakes expire after 7 days if not completed.
  • Once minted, the blockchain record is permanent and immutable. It cannot be reversed, edited, or deleted by us or any party.
  • Handshakes award 10 points to each party upon completion.
